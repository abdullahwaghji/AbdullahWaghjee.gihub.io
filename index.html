About Us

Established in 2006, Moon Textiles is a modern fabric manufacturing company headquartered in Karachi, Pakistan. The company specializes in developing an extensive range of woven and knit fabrics for the apparel industry. All our fabric is manufactured on demand. Apart from Global retail brands and their respective converters, we cater to garment manufacturers and private entities who are looking to source high quality finished fabric. Moon Textiles grows stronger with each day. Our mill employs over 200 people who have shown exemplary skill and helped the company export over 5 million meters of fabric to date.Our shared experience helps us provide our clients with customised fabric of exceptional quality. Get in Touch WOVEN & KNITS Made-to-order Fabrics Moon Textiles offers unique fabric solutions to people in the apparel & textile industry. After carefully understanding the physical & technical aspects of the garment, we prepare its respective fabric on-demand.
